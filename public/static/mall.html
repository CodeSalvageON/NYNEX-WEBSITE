MS DOS Virtual Mall Edition

Please, do scroll down and look at the history of what is now this site, and the up to date projects you see on the main page.

The above website, "CodeSalvageWeb", was an early example of the current edition, and it was made on June 10th, 2018, 9:17 AM.

"TSpeedyLife" was an updated version of "CodeSalvageWeb", with basic CSS and more complex CSS from various tutorial websites. It was made on July 21st, 2018, at 8:14 PM.

"TSBD HTML 5 Edition" was an attempt at making a sort of social media website, and used Google Document embeds due to server networking knowledge being virtually non existant to CodeSalvageON at the time. It was created on January 8th, 2020, at 9:56 PM.

"One Neighborhood" was a website designed to act as a online multiplayer building simulator. It was made on March 4th, 2020, 2:08 PM.

"Primitive Reddit" was a primitive version of Reddit written in PHP, and it only had two core features, although it was the first CodeSalvageON/NYNEX website to have proper XSS protection, and was made on March 24, 2020, at 10:32 PM.

"Render Land" was one of the first CodeSalvageON websites that used the NodeJS framework. It was made on June 11, 2020, at 12:59 PM. Around this time, Email Town was built as well.

"The Warroom" was a proof-of-concept image board idea originally made as a school project. It was made on September 16th, 2020, at 2:09 PM.

The "ASP Archives" was a website made as a tribute and as an archive for the now defunct Minecraft server, anarchy.survival.pe. The website was created on September 28th, 2020, at 10:12 PM.
